Legend

Colour intensity is calculated as the sum of bump or stop severity and frequency. The larger the bump or the more abrupt the stop, the higher the value assigned to it. Red signifies the greates problem areas, green minor problems.

About

TranSeNS is a bachelor thesis project done in conjunction with Imperial College London in part fulfilment of the requirements of the degree of Bachelor of Engineering.

All data is gathered using the TranSeNS Android app built specifically for this project.
